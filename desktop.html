Easy Grape Jam

Ingredients:

  • 1.7 kg seedless grapes
  • 1 kg sugar
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Place grapes, sugar and lemon juice in a pan.
  2. Heat gently and stir until sugar dissolves.
  3. Crush grapes to release juice.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Bring to a rolling boil for about 25 minutes.
  6. Test on a cold plate.
  7. If it wrinkles, it's ready.
  8. Pour into jars and seal.
